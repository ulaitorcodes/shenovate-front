What we do

Shenovate aims to close this gender gap by promoting and inspiring women to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). At shenovate, we are dedicated to enhancing STEM education and support for girls by offering free world-class training, mentorship, internship and job placements, programs, and other resources to attract, employ and retain women in STEM fields.
